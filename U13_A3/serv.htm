Services

What services do we offer?

  • Device sales: We offer a curated selection of smartphones, basic phones, laptops and accessories for purchase.
  • Phone repairs: We provide top of the line, in-house repair services for any issue. From more common ones such as screen replacements and software troubleshooting all the way to repairing water damage.
  • Technical support: We assist our loyal customers with device setup, software updates and addressing basic technical issues.
  • Unlocking services: We help customers unlock their phones.
  • SIM card services: Providing and assisting customers with SIM card activation for new or existing phone numbers.
  • Prepaid plans: Offering prepaid phone plans and services for customers who prefer a pay-as-you-go approach.
  • Trade in programs: Allowing customers to trade in their old devices for credit toward the purchase of a new device or accessories.
  • Bill payments:Facilitating bill payments for mobile services, allowing customers to pay their monthly bills in-store.
  • Used device sales: Selling pre-owned or refurbished devices at a lower cost than brand-new devices.
