About me

Hi there, I'm Chiara Elliotte. I'm studying Level 2 ICT at Coleg Cambria Deeside, and it's been quite the adventure! I've always been fascinated by technology and how it can change our lives. Even though the coursework can be tough, I face each challenge with determination and a positive attitude.
